Ohio Property Tax Information 2026

Average Effective Rate: 1.82% (above national average) County + Municipal + School + Special

Assessment Ratio: Residential property assessed at 35% of market value.

Key Exemptions & Credits:

  • Homestead Exemption: $25,000 reduction for seniors 65+ and disabled homeowners
  • Rollback Credit: 10% credit on tax bill (state pays 10%) + 2.5% credit (state pays 2.5%)
  • Owner-Occupancy Credit: 2.5% credit for primary residences

Tax Composition: School districts ~55%, Municipal ~25%, County ~15%, Special districts ~5%

Source: Ohio Department of Taxation — Updated July 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Ohio property tax rate?
Ohio's average effective rate is 1.82%, ranging from 0.8% in some counties to over 2.5% in others.
What is the Homestead Exemption in Ohio?
The Homestead Exemption provides a $25,000 reduction in assessed value for homeowners 65+ or disabled, saving approximately $400–$600 per year.
