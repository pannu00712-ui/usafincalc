Frequently Asked Questions — Maryland Paycheck

How much is $60,000 a year after taxes in Maryland?
On a $60,000 salary in Maryland (single, Prince George's County): Take-home ~$46,137/year ($3,845/month).
What is the Maryland state income tax rate for 2026?
Maryland uses 8 brackets: 2.0%, 3.0%, 4.0%, 4.75%, 5.0%, 5.25%, 5.5%, and 5.75%.
