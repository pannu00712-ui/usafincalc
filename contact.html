What Helps Us Help You Faster

For calculation errors, the most useful thing you can include is your specific inputs and what result you got versus what you expected. "The mortgage calculator seems wrong" is hard to trace. "I entered $450k loan, 30 years, 6.8% — it showed $2,940/month but I'm getting a different number elsewhere" is something we can actually investigate. Same applies for methodology questions: the more specific, the faster we can dig in.

Privacy and Your Message

Any information you send through this form is used only to respond to you. We don't add people to email lists without asking, and we don't share contact details with anyone for marketing. That's it. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Faster Answers Are Sometimes Already Here

We get a lot of the same questions, and a few pages exist specifically to answer them. Before sending a message, it's worth checking:

  • Methodology — how each calculator works and exactly which data it uses
  • Data Sources — every IRS, SSA, and BLS reference, with publication numbers
  • Disclaimer — the important limitations of what these tools can and can't tell you
