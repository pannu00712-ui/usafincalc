What Our Calculators Can and Can't Do

A few specific limitations worth knowing about before you rely on a result:

  • Tax calculators: These don't model all AMT scenarios, don't capture every state/local tax situation, and assume the standard deduction unless you enter itemized inputs. If your tax situation is complex, treat our results as a starting point.
  • Mortgage calculators: We show principal + interest. Actual loan costs vary by lender — origination fees, points, HOA dues, and exact escrow amounts aren't included. Get a Loan Estimate from a real lender before committing.
  • Retirement calculators: We use a default 7% return assumption based on historical averages. Future returns aren't guaranteed, and we don't model sequence-of-returns risk. The real world is messier than any projection.
  • Investment calculators: Results are pre-tax. Taxes on capital gains and dividends within taxable accounts aren't factored in.
