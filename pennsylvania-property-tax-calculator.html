Pennsylvania Property Tax Information 2026

Average Effective Rate: 1.58% (above national average) County + Municipal + School

Key Exemptions:

  • Homestead Exemption: Available in most counties (varies by county, typically $30,000–$50,000 reduction)
  • Senior Citizen Tax Relief: Rebates for homeowners 65+ with income under $35,000
  • Disabled Veterans: Exemption for 100% disabled veterans

Tax Composition: School districts ~50%, Municipal ~30%, County ~20%

Source: Pennsylvania Department of Revenue — Updated July 2026

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Pennsylvania property tax rate?
Pennsylvania's average effective rate is 1.58%, ranging from 0.5% in some counties to over 2.5% in others. Rates are set by county, municipality, and school district.
What is the Homestead Exemption in PA?
The Homestead Exemption reduces assessed value for primary residences. The amount varies by county, typically $30,000–$50,000 off assessed value, saving $300–$800 per year.
