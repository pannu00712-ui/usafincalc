Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a percentage?
To find X% of Y, multiply Y by X/100. For example, 20% of 200 = 200 × 0.20 = 40.
How do I calculate percentage increase?
Percentage increase = ((New - Original) / Original) × 100. For example, from 100 to 120 = ((120-100)/100) × 100 = 20%.
