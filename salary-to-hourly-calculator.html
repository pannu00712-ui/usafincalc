How to Use This Calculator

Switch between Salary → Hourly and Hourly → Salary modes using the toggle. Enter your annual salary or hourly rate, along with your typical hours per week and weeks worked per year. The calculator instantly shows your equivalent hourly rate or annual salary, plus monthly, weekly, biweekly, and overtime pay.

Use the Quick Presets to test common salaries or hourly wages. The Visual Comparison bar chart shows the relationship between annual and hourly figures, and the Time-Based Breakdown shows your pay at different time intervals.

Formulas

Salary to Hourly: Hourly Rate = Annual Salary ÷ (Hours per Week × Weeks per Year)

Hourly to Salary: Annual Salary = Hourly Rate × Hours per Week × Weeks per Year

Overtime Rate: Hourly Rate × 1.5 (standard overtime multiplier)

Monthly: Annual Salary ÷ 12 | Weekly: Annual Salary ÷ 52 | Biweekly: Annual Salary ÷ 26

Common Use Cases

  • Job offer evaluation: Compare a salaried offer to an hourly position.
  • Freelance / contract work: Determine what hourly rate equals your target annual income.
  • Budgeting: Understand your income at different time intervals.
  • Overtime planning: See how overtime pay increases your effective hourly rate.
